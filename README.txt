*** EXCAVATE ***


*** Launch ***

For intended display, run included excavate.bat file to launch excavate.py in Command Prompt.


*** Scenario ***

During a mining expedition, you find yourself trapped in a cave, surrounded by falling rocks.
Thankfully, with your pickaxe, you stand a fighting chance of survival.

Reach the target score to win.

If the screen fills up with rocks, you lose.


*** Controls ***

Use WASD to move.

Press I to smash rocks.
